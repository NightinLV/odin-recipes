Ajapsandali

Description

Ajapsandali is a traditional Georgian and Armenian dish, also popular in the Northern Caucasus. It consists of onion, eggplant, tomato and bell pepper grilled, stewed, or fried in vegetable oil and seasoned with garlic, basil, coriander leaves, parsley and other seasoning.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggplants
  • 2 onions
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 hot pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 bunch coriander
  • 1 bunch purple basil
  • red hot chilli flakes to taste
  • salt to taste
  • sunflower oil for frying

Instructions:

Step 1:

Cut the eggplants lengthwise or into cubes. Sprinkle with some salt and leave in a bowl about 20-30 minutes. Then rinse and squeeze well to rid the eggplants of some of itâ€™s bitterness. Cut peppers and onions into thin strips. Peel and cut tomatoes. Pour 3-4 tablespoons of oil on a heated pan and fry the eggplant. When golden, add onion and pepper. Cover and simmer on medium heat for 10 minutes, stir from time to time. When the vegetables are softened and cooked, add the tomatoes and cook for another 5 minutes. Finally add the crushed garlic, finely chopped herbs, salt and hot pepper.
